Summer Berry Salad

Summer 25 minutes Easy Serves 4
Vegetarian Gluten-Free

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, lettuce)
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 2 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry all greens and berries thoroughly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens with the sliced strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries.
  3. Sprinkle the crumbled goat cheese and sliced almonds over the top.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic glaze, salt, and pepper.
  5. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving and toss gently to combine.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy this refreshing summer dish!

Nutrition Information

250 Calories
18g Fat
22g Carbs
5g Protein

Chef's Notes

For a vegan version, substitute the goat cheese with avocado slices or vegan cheese. You can also add grilled chicken or salmon for extra protein.
